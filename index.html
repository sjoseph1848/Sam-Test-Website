Sam's Journey to Become a Software Engineer

The Mission

I have a strong belief that in order to be successful in the 21st century one must possess skills in the following areas:

  1. Business
  2. Engineering
  3. Technology

If you understand technology you can bring your imagination to reality. If you couple technology with engineering you understand the important concepts of building things(whether they be physical or virtual). When you add business to this trio of skills you are then able to profit from the ideas you had previously brought to life. I have made it my life goal to achieve these three skills.
